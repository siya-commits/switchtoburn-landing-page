The missing piece

You haven't failed the diet.
The diet failed you.

Here's what most health advice doesn't account for: during perimenopause and menopause, declining oestrogen directly reduces insulin sensitivity. Your cells become less efficient at moving glucose into the body for energy — and your body gets locked into sugar-burning mode.

A sugar-burning body can't access stored fat efficiently. So it stores more of it — especially around the abdomen. Energy crashes. Cravings spike. And the calorie restriction and extra cardio that used to work? On a stressed, hormonally shifting body, they often make things worse.

🔬

This is biology, not willpower

The tools you've been using were designed for a different hormonal environment. Restoring metabolic flexibility — your body's ability to switch between burning glucose and burning fat — is the missing piece. And it's exactly what the 90-Day Switch Reset is built to do.
